Staying up to date with IT trends, news, and best practices is time-consuming work, particularly if you’re busy running a small business. But, being aware of emerging technologies in 2023 can give you a competitive advantage and help accelerate business growth. The key is to rely on trusted resources that consistently deliver the knowledge you need.

The Top 15 Trending IT Blogs to Follow in 2023

To help you keep up with the latest developments in the world of IT, we’ve compiled a list of the top 15 technology blogs that every business leader and decision-maker should follow.

1. Electric

Obviously we’re biased, but Electric strives to cover everything IT-related for both technical and non-technical readers. Whether you’re a CEO, HR manager, Ops team lead, or IT professional, we’ve got the best practices you need to know when it comes to tech trends, cybersecurity, remote support, and the employee IT experience. Head over to our webinars, white papers, and case studies for even more insights. We’re not the only resource out there though, below are some of the other great IT blogs that we love and follow ourselves.

 

2. CIO Review

The CIO Review blog provides readers with comprehensive technology news and insights across a wide spectrum of topics. Aimed at C-Level and senior management, you’ll find resources on blockchain, big data, cloud migration, data analytics, robotics, and much more. Don’t miss their case studies on the best-performing technology companies.

3. IT Brew

IT Brew shares the latest news and analysis of trends shaping the IT industry, particularly within the areas of cybersecurity, big data, software development, remote work, cloud computing, and data privacy. Delivered in an easily digestible format and style, their daily newsletter is the perfect resource for all levels of IT knowledge and expertise.

4. Sentinel Labs

In the rapidly evolving world of InfoSec, Sentinel Labs is where you’ll find highly-informed deep dives into the latest cybersecurity dangers and developments. Threat researchers and vetted contributors share critical information on malware, exploits, cybercrime, and APTs, with the aim of contributing to a safer digital space for everyone.

5. Wired

Wired is a popular tech blog that observes how technology affects human lives across culture, politics, and security. Business leaders can access informative news, delivered through approachable, easy-to-understand content. Focused on breakthroughs and innovation, you’ll also find hot takes on modern life, tech trends, and gadget reviews.

6. MIT Technology Review

The MIT Technology Review was founded in 1899 and provides insights, reviews, analyses, and interviews on new technologies. Read up on how IT impacts people all over the world from a commercial, political, and social perspective. Specialist topics covered include AI, climate change, biotechnology, and computing.

7. Fast Company

Fast Company is one of the top business media brands, with an editorial focus on technological innovation, leadership, and world-changing ideas. Aimed at progressive business leaders, the blog’s stated purpose is to inspire readers to think expansively, lead with purpose, embrace change, and shape the future of business.

8. Mashable

Covering everything from art and culture to science and tech, Mashable offers in-depth news, reviews, and advice in each of its sub-categories. Subscribe to stay up to date on digital culture trends and the latest apps, software, social media, cybersecurity, and mobile developments.

9. TechCrunch

TechCrunch is a popular blog covering startups, business apps, digital companies, and emerging issues in IT. Their members-only community, TechCrunch+ is an invaluable resource for founders and growing teams to keep up with market analysis, fundraising advice, and SMB growth hacks.

10. Gizmodo

Gizmodo has been around for over two decades and is one of the best places to find expert reviews of new gadgets as they hit the market. You’ll also find insightful news on privacy, environmental policy, and labor, among other subjects. Known to serve up tech knowledge with a side of humor, it’s highly respected for its honest and transparent content.

11. Engadget

Engadget has morphed from a news organization into a top tech technology blog over the last few years. As a business manager, you can catch up with all the latest tech, gaming, and entertainment news and reviews, or search for past tech publications from Engadget’s archives. The blog even reviews NASA’s tech and has an independent video section.

12. The Verge

The Verge examines how technology affects all of our lives – both now and into the future. Created for a mainstream audience, the blog presents engaging content with a straightforward approach. Find news, podcasts, feature stories, guidebooks, product reviews, and more among their resources.

13. VentureBeat

VentureBeat focuses on transformative tech innovations and breaking news, and is ideal for founders and developers alike. A leading authority on artificial intelligence and machine learning, it also offers incisive coverage of the gaming industry and promises to equip business leaders to make smart decisions.

14. CNET

CNET is a long-running tech publisher that “tells you what’s new and why it matters.” Populated with news, reviews, and deep dives, the site’s content is intended to help readers make informed decisions about the tech they use and buy. In more recent years, their content has also expanded to cover wellness and financial advice.
